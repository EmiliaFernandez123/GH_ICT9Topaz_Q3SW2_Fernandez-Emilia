FROZEN
 Frozen is movie directed by Jennifer Lee and Chris Buck, it revolves around two sisters. Elsa, one with ice powers, and her sister Anna who she's unfortunately cursed when they were young children. The movie showcases how Elsa come sto terms with her strong powers, and how the bond between family and sisters are able to help you in self-stability, which I think is an incredible message to young kids watching. November 27, 2013
HOTEL TRANSYLVANIA 2 Hotel Transylvania, a movie directed by Genndy Tartakovsky, is a second movie from the first version of Hotel Transylvania. This movie now revolves around the plot of Dracula's acceptance of humans, but yet is faced with the concern that his grandson maybe be a human. This in itself showcases that Dracula still isn't fully accepting of humans coinciding with monsters, steming from his father which later appears in the movie. The slow acceptance of humanity in his grandson and his father's great grandson is what makes this movie heart-warming to me. September 28, 2012
WHITE CHICKS White Chicks, a comedy based film directed by Keenen Ivory Wayans, follows the plot of two FBI agent brothers, having to disguise themselves as two famous white sisters due to a mission. This movie is incredibly light-hearted and meant for laughs, while still having emotional scenes. We follow their hilarious journey of deception and efforts to complete this mission. October 20, 2004
HOW TO TRAIN YOUR DRAGON: THE HIDDEN WORLD How to Train Your Dragon: The Hidden World direced by Dean DeBlois, is the third movie from the original How to Train Your Dragon. This movie, while already having established their world from the second movie, showcases a new Night Fury, which is now white instead of black. This discovery intrigues people in their land, and urge them to discover more. When they find out a dragon keeper who's planning to wipe out all other dragon kind, Hiccup and the cast seek to save Toothless and other dragons. February 20, 2019
THE CAT RETURNS Hiroyuki Morita, the director of The Cat Returns, directed a light-hearted movie with plot that revolves around a highschool girl and a cat. She rescues this cat, only to be run over by a truck and brought to the cat's world, where she discovers the almost fairytale setting of it. July 20, 2002
